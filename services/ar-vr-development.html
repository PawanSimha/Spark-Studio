What is
AR & VR?

Augmented Reality (AR) overlays digital information onto the real world, typically viewed through a smartphone or tablet camera. Virtual Reality (VR) creates a completely simulated, three-dimensional environment that a user can interact with via a headset. Both technologies offer unique opportunities to train, entertain, and educate in ways never before possible.
