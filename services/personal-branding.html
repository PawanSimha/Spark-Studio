What is
Personal Branding?

Personal branding is the conscious and strategic effort to create and influence public perception of an individual. It's the practice of marketing yourself and your career as a brand. A strong personal brand helps you stand out, build trust with your audience, and attract new opportunities. It's about showcasing your unique skills, experience, and personality to a wider audience.
