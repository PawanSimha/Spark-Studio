Our Standard

The Success Framework

We don't just design for aesthetics; we design for impact. Our rigorous methodology ensures every digital product we launch is optimized for usability, engagement, and business growth.

Usability Validation

Measuring how effectively and efficiently users can complete their goals within your interface.

  • Task Success Rate (TSR)
  • Average Time on Task
  • User Error Rate Analysis
  • System Usability Scale (SUS)

Engagement Metrics

Analyzing user behavior to understand how they interact with and navigate through the product.

  • Dwell Time & Bounce Rate
  • Click-Through Rate (CTR)
  • Scroll Depth & Heatmaps
  • Interactions Per Visit (IPV)

Technical Performance

Ensuring the system functions correctly and at peak speeds across all devices and networks.

  • Google Core Web Vitals
  • Largest Contentful Paint (LCP)
  • Time to Interactive (TTI)
  • Error Log & Crash Rate Monitoring

Inclusion & Compliance

Verifying that the product is accessible to everyone, regardless of ability or technology.

  • WCAG 2.1 Compliance (AA/AAA)
  • Automated Accessibility Scoring
  • Screen Reader Compatibility
  • Color Contrast Ratio Validation

Loyalty & Sentiment

Understanding how users feel about the product through direct feedback and loyalty metrics.

  • Net Promoter Score (NPS)
  • Customer Satisfaction (CSAT)
  • Customer Effort Score (CES)
  • Ongoing User Sentiment Analysis

Business Results

Connecting design performance directly to your bottom line and long-term business goals.

  • Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)
  • Customer Retention & Churn Rate
  • Cart Abandonment Analysis
  • Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) Improvement

Why We Measure Everything

In the digital landscape, what can't be measured can't be improved. At Spark Studio, we believe that the highest form of creativity is problem-solving. By integrating these specific validation and verification metrics into our workflow, we provide our clients with a clear view of their ROI.
