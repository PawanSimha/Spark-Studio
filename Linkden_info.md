# LinkedIn Project: Spark Studio

**Project name**
Spark Studio: Creative Digital Agency Platform

**Description**
Developed a premium, cinematic digital agency platform specializing in branding, design, and immersive technology. The project features an Apple-inspired glassmorphism aesthetic and a high-performance static Multi-Page Application (MPA) architecture. By leveraging semantic HTML5, modern CSS techniques (Tailwind & Vanilla), and ES6+ JavaScript, I created a seamless, responsive experience optimized for both human users and AI/search engine discovery.

The platform includes a cinematic hero section, interactive SVG-based visualizations (custom India map), and dynamic component injection for improved maintainability (DRY principles). I also implemented comprehensive SEO metadata using JSON-LD (Schema.org), Open Graph, and Twitter Cards, ensuring maximum visibility and indexing across search engines and LLMs.

**Top 5 skills used in this project**
1. UI/UX Design (Cinematic Glassmorphism & Responsive Web Design)
2. Front-End Development (Semantic HTML5, CSS3, & ES6+ JavaScript)
3. SEO & AI Optimization (JSON-LD, Schema.org, & Meta Tagging)
4. Performance Engineering (LCP Optimization & Static Site Scalability)
5. Brand Identity & Content Strategy (Digital Marketing & Media Production)
